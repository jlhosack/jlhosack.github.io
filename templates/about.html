Come see about me!

Julia is a Bay Area transplant residing in New York City. She has been on the road with multiple touring productions and has performed in 46 out of the 50 states (including Alaska and Hawaii). She is happiest when performing, but when she is not onstage, she can be found doing the three "C"s: crafting, cooking, and cooter slamming.


Julia holds a BFA in Musical Theatre from Notre Dame de Namur University and has studied and performed with Operafestival di Roma
